[Suppliers & logos]

- Flight section: logo from carrier (WestJet), NOT supplier (Sunwing)
- getAirlineLogo() in api.js; westjet → westjet_quebec logo alias

[Taxes]
- NO auto TPS/TVQ — uses GHL fields only
- taxes_occ_* = TOTAL tax amount per occupation (not per person)
- taxes_par_personne sent to GHL form = total taxes ÷ nb voyageurs (internal calc)

[Occupation pricing — CRITICAL RULE]
Client enters in GHL per occupation:
  price / price_occ_*  = total BEFORE taxes for the full occupation
  taxes_occ_*          = total TAXES for the full occupation
Frontend displays:
  Table: Avant taxes | Taxes | Total (occupation totals, NOT per person)
  Picker: total with taxes + breakdown "dont X avant taxes + Y taxes"
  Summary after selection: occupation label + "Total estimé" + dépôt only
NO per-person multiplication on the frontend.

[Children — backend only, NO frontend selector]
- NO Enfants +/- UI on product page (removed)
- KEEP price/tax details on occupation options and summary box
- Child counts for GHL form from occupation definition (nombre_enfants_2_12, etc.)
- GHL fields: price_child_2_12, price_child_13_17, tax_child_2_12, tax_child_13_17
