FEATURES COMPLETED IN RECENT SESSIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[Suppliers]
- SUPPLIER_LABELS + SUPPLIER_LOGOS in config.js
- Logos on listing cards, product header, supplier filter, flight section
- Flight logo uses carrier (WestJet), NOT supplier (Sunwing) — getAirlineLogo()
- westjet → westjet_quebec logo alias

[Taxes]
- NO auto TPS/TVQ calculation
- Per-occupation taxes from GHL: taxes_occ_double, taxes_occ_triple, etc.
- Occupation totals model (see below)

[Occupation pricing — IMPORTANT]
Client enters TOTALS per occupation in GHL (not per person):
  price / price_occ_*     = total BEFORE taxes for full occupation
  taxes_occ_*             = total TAX amount for full occupation
Site displays:
  Avant taxes | Taxes | Total (occupation totals)
  taxes_par_personne = total taxes ÷ number of travelers (for GHL form)

[Children]
- UI on product.html: +/- for enfants 2-12 and 13-17
- Fields: price_child_2_12, price_child_13_17, tax_child_2_12, tax_child_13_17
- Sent to GHL form: nombre_enfants_2_12, nombre_enfants_13_17, etc.

[Filters]
- Dynamic supplier filter from products
- Departure date filter (from product dates)
- Destination filter with normalizeDestinationKey() — case/slug insensitive
  (e.g. GHL "bahamas" matches filter "Bahamas")
- Star filter includes 4.5
- Destination alias: jamaque → Jamaïque

[UI]
- Rabais badge: red (bg-red-600 on cards, text-red-600 on product)
- forfait_link → "Voir le forfait original" on hotel tab

[iframe embed]
- embed.js + ?embed=1 hides hero/countdown in iframe
- integration-iframe.html — auto-resize iframe snippet for client sites
- config.js: BOUTIQUE_EMBED_URL = .../index.html?embed=1

[GHL form prefill]
- buildGhlReservationParams() in api.js
- GHL_FORM_IFRAME_KEYS in config.js (limited keys for URL length)
- Form URL: GHL_FORM_EMBED_URL in config.js
